About Us

MVM Construction, LLC. are General Contractors and Construction Managers. Years of experience makes us well qualified and equipped to handle large and small scale projects, both Residential and Commercial, with much of our work coming from repeat clients. MVM’s work includes Paving, Site Work, Demolition, Excavating and Building. We closely manage the construction project from start to finish ensuring that each project is completed in an efficient manner with a strong focus on quality.

MVM is committed to the success and increased adoption of construction practices throughout the industry. As part of this effort, we are starting the practice of green building, emphasizing the LEED rating system.

Core Values:

Team Work MVM recognizes our primary asset is people. To be successful, MVM must be a rewarding place to work. We provide opportunity and encouragement to help our peers reach their potential. We work with our client as a team. Mutual respect provides the foundation for our success.
Integrity MVM remains true to our founding values of quality, honesty and hard work. MVM upholds the highest ethical standard in the industry.
Safety MVM has developed an atmosphere that promotes an injury-free environment and provides the safest workplace possible for our employees, subcontractors, clients and others who enter or who approach our construction sites.

MVM is consistently associated with the high standards of service, quality, personal attention to clients, and integrity. MVM was formed in 1990 by Gino Secchiano. Throughout his career, Gino Secchiano referred to all clients as friends. The company quickly built its reputation for integrity, team work and commitment. These values remain true to date and are the reason MVM has been in business for 20 years. Our position and strength enable the company to provide additional value to our clients and opportunities for our employees.
